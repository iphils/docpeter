Serving Maradu · Kundannoor · Nettoor

An internal medicine physician, a short drive from Maradu.

For residents of Maradu, Kundannoor and Nettoor — including the apartment communities along NH-66 — Dr. Peter Prince Pokkattu offers careful, personalised internal medicine consultations from his residence in Thripunithura.

Getting here from Maradu

The clinic is roughly 4 km from Maradu — about 10 minutes via Kundannoor or NH-66 into Petta and on to Thripunithura. Evening hours from 4 PM to 9 PM, every day, suit working professionals and families alike.

Why patients from Maradu visit

  • Long-format consultations — diagnosis is never rushed
  • Diabetes, hypertension and thyroid follow-up under one physician
  • Allergy diagnosis and treatment, including allergy injections
  • Geriatric care for elderly parents at home
  • Consults in English, Malayalam, Hindi and Tamil

Conditions commonly seen

Routine illness, fevers, ongoing chronic care, allergies, pre-employment and pre-travel checks, and preventive screening for adults.
