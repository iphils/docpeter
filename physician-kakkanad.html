An evening physician for Kakkanad residents.

If you live or work in Kakkanad and are looking for a careful, unhurried physician for an evening consultation, Dr. Peter Prince Pokkattu has been practising internal medicine in Kochi for over 17 years. The clinic runs from his residence in Thripunithura, an easy drive from Infopark, SmartCity, KINFRA and the wider Kakkanad area.

Getting here from Kakkanad

Thripunithura is roughly 12 km from Kakkanad — about 25–35 minutes in evening traffic. Most patients use the Seaport-Airport Road down to Vyttila junction, then NH-66 / Petta into Thripunithura. Evening slots from 4 PM to 9 PM are designed to suit Infopark and SmartCity professionals who finish work between 5 and 7 PM.
